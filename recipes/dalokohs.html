Dalokohs Bar

Description

Adds +50 max health for 30 seconds

Eat to gain up to 100 health.

Alt-fire: Share chocolate with a friend

(Small Health Kit)

Ingredients

  • 100g cacao butter
  • 30g raw cacao powder
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 25g milk powder

Steps

  1. Melt cacao butter until liquid and set aside
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a medium-sized bowl
  3. Gently pour pure maple syrup and melted cacao butter into the bowl
  4. Mix ingredients until smooth and all chunks have been removed
  5. Pour mixture into a chocolate mold and tap to remove air bubbles
  6. Place mold into a refrigerator for 3 hours
  7. Carefully remove solid chocolate from the mold
  8. Enjoy!
