The Sandvich

Description

Eat to regain up to 300 health.

Alt-fire: Share a Sandvich with a friend

(Medium Health Kit)

Ingredients

  • 2 traingular slices of bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Swiss cheese
  • Ham
  • Bologna
  • 1 green pimento-stuffed olive

Steps

  1. Lay down one triangular slice of bread
  2. Layer on ham, bologna, swiss cheese, tomato, and lettuce
  3. Top with second triangular slice of bread
  4. Pin the green pimento-stuffed olive to the top of The Sandvich with a toothpick
  5. Enjoy!
